Welcome to China Horizon Forum

Co-organized by the French-Chinese Friendship Association of ParisTech (AFCP) and ParisTech, Forum Horizon Chine is a space for recruiting French sinophile and Chinese students and graduates from the engineering and business Grandes Écoles of ParisTech.

This year, the sixth edition will be held at Mines ParisTech on April 18, 2013 at 10 am. This unique event attracts the most promising students and graduates interested in a French-Chinese career. It will include a job fair and conference facilities where you will:

  • Meet talented young Chinese students and graduates as well as French students and graduates wishing to pursue a career in China,
  • Present your company and your development strategy in China and Asia,
  • Communicate with other companies and representatives of China’s special economic zones, in order to know the Chinese market, its regulatory environment and its opportunities for implementation.

You will participate in a series of conferences and round tables at the end of the day followed by a cocktail.

This forum is co-organized by the French-Chinese Friendship Association ParisTech (AFCP) and ParisTech, with the support of the Embassy of China.

You are a student or an alumnus of Paristech?

You can now register on the registration page. Find out more about this Forum, in "Visitor" section, you can take a look at the program and at the companies that will participate in.

Wish to get a stand for your company?

Please do not hesitate to contact us to receive our offers. In section "Exhibitors", You will find a presentation of the visitors of the Forum as well as the services offered.
